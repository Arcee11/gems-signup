What's In The Program

Everything your agent needs to coach you.

Six layers. Each one doing something different. Together, a complete coaching system — running on any AI agent, on any device, for the rest of your life.

Emotional Intelligence FREE

A clinically aligned emotional intelligence layer built into every session. Your agent recognizes what you're carrying — anxiety, exhaustion, grief, frustration — names it without projecting, and guides you through it before moving into the day's challenge. Included at every tier, at no cost.

40 Quality Curriculum

Resilience, focus, gratitude, confidence — 40 human qualities, each a full coaching module. Context, challenges, a path forward, and a suggested next quality when you're ready to move on.

Sound Sanctuary

Music matched to your quality, your emotional state, and the day's energy. Your agent opens the Sanctuary at the start of every session. The sound layer of the program — and it goes deeper at higher tiers.

The Compass

A 60-card deck — 36 Action, 24 Self-Care. Your agent draws from the Compass at key moments in your challenge week and interprets each card in the context of where you are. Physical deck available as an expansion.

The Voyager Game

Quests, XP, coins, and 10 levels from Seeker to Legend. Narrative arcs that turn each quality week into a chapter in a story you're living. Growth with stakes — and real milestones when you hit them.

Community

An invisible social layer — blind leaderboards, community challenges, and real-time stats your agent weaves into sessions naturally. No feeds. No followers. Just a sense you're not doing this alone.

New content, new qualities, new expansions — GEMS is continuously updated.
