How It Works

From signup to first session in minutes.

01

Subscribe

Choose your tier, complete checkout. You'll receive an email with your GitHub access invitation and setup instructions.

02

Connect your agent

Accept the GitHub invitation. Then tell your AI agent (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any other) to read the GEMS repository. That's it.

03

Scan or choose a quality

Scan a QR code from the book — or tell your agent which quality you want to work. The agent reads the content and your coaching session begins.

04

Show up daily

Check in with your agent every day. Music plays, challenges unfold, XP accumulates. Seven days at a time. Forty qualities over a lifetime.

After you subscribe — what to do next

1

Check your email

Within 24 hours, you'll receive a GitHub collaboration invitation. Accept it — this grants your account access to the GEMS repository for your tier.

2

Create a free GitHub account (if you don't have one)

Go to github.com → Sign up → confirm email. Your username is what you entered above. It takes about 2 minutes.

3

Tell your AI agent to read GEMS

Open your AI agent (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any other) and say:

Read the GEMS repository at github.com/Arcee11/gems-core and let's begin my first quality challenge.

For Pro/Max tiers, your agent will automatically have access to additional repos.

4

Scan your book QR code (if you have the book)

Open the camera on any device, scan the QR code next to the quality you want to start, and your agent will navigate directly to that quality's content.

5

Your first session begins

Your agent will read the GEMS content, introduce the quality, stream your opening music, and give you your first challenge. Show up again tomorrow. That's how the journey works.
