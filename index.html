After you subscribe — what to do next

1

Check your email

Within 24 hours, you'll receive a GitHub collaboration invitation. Accept it — this grants your account access to the GEMS program for your tier.

2

Create a free GitHub account (if you don't have one)

Go to github.com → Sign up → confirm email. Your username is what you entered above. Takes about 2 minutes.

3

Tell your AI agent to read GEMS

Open your AI agent (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any other) and say:

Read the GEMS program at github.com/Arcee11/gems-core and let's begin my first quality challenge.

Pro and Max subscribers: your agent will automatically have access to the deeper repos.

4

Tell your agent which quality calls to you — or ask it to recommend one based on where you are right now. If you have the book expansion, scan a QR code and your agent navigates directly there.

5

Your first session begins

Your agent reads the program, welcomes you into your quality, opens the Sound Sanctuary, and gives you your first challenge. Show up again tomorrow. That's how the program works.
